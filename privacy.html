Privacy Policy – Taxi Idle Game: City Builder

Effective Date: 10/01/2025

Your privacy is important. This Privacy Policy explains how Taxi Idle Game: City Builder handles information when you use our mobile game available on the Apple App Store.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information from users of our game.

  • No account creation is required.
  • We do not track your location, device ID, or any other identifying information.
  • We do not use analytics tools or third-party tracking.

The game offers optional in-app purchases (e.g., Gems, VIP content, removing ads).

All purchases are processed securely by Apple App Store. We do not have access to your payment details (such as credit card numbers or Apple ID information).

3. Children’s Privacy

Our game is intended for a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13. If you believe we may have unintentionally collected information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

4. Third-Party Services

The game may rely on the Apple App Store for distribution and payments. These services are subject to Apple’s own privacy policies.

5. Your Privacy Rights

Since no data is collected, there are no data access, modification, or deletion requests needed. However, if you have any questions or concerns, you can contact us.

6. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app or on the App Store page with a revised “Effective Date”.
